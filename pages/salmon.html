Ingredients

  • 1 salmon filet (with or without skin)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat iron skillet over a gas stove
  2. Rub Salmon with olive oil and coat with salt and pepper
  3. Add salmon skin side down (if there is skin) and cook for 4 minutes
  4. flip salmon and cook for an additional 4 minutes
  5. Check temp to be sure internal temp is at least 145ÂºF
  6. remove from skillet and let rest for 2 minutes before serving
